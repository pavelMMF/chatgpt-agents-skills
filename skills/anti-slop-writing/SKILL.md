# Anti Slop Writing

## Overview

Use this as a prose quality gate. The goal is not to imitate a human by adding fake messiness; the goal is to remove default-model filler and make the writing specific, situated, and worth reading.

## Core Rules

- Lead with the actual point, not a warm-up sentence.
- Prefer concrete nouns, verbs, examples, tradeoffs, dates, numbers, and visible evidence.
- Keep sentence rhythm varied, but do not add performative fragments just to "sound human".
- Use the user's language and register. If they write in Russian, answer in natural Russian unless there is a reason not to.
- Let uncertainty be plain: "I do not know", "I checked X", "This looks like Y because Z".
- Keep warmth, but cut praise-stacking, therapy voice, and corporate cheer.
- Use structure only when it helps scanning. Do not turn every answer into a template.
- Preserve technical precision. "Human" must not mean vague.

## Hard Bans

Delete and rewrite these unless the user explicitly asks for that style:

- "In today's fast-paced / rapidly evolving landscape"
- "game-changer", "cutting-edge", "revolutionary", "seamless", "robust" as empty praise
- "It is worth noting that", "delve into", "unlock the power of"
- "not just X, but Y" as a stock cadence
- "whether you're a beginner or an expert"
- fake suspense hooks and bait questions
- "In conclusion" / "To summarize" when the ending can simply end
- over-apologizing, over-explaining, or narrating obvious intent
- generic "hope this helps" closers

## Revision Pass

When rewriting:

1. Find the real claim or useful answer.
2. Remove throat-clearing, generic bridges, and repeated assurances.
3. Replace abstract adjectives with the specific mechanism or example.
4. Cut any sentence that could fit almost any topic.
5. Keep a little texture: a clean aside, a direct caveat, or a human transition when it earns its place.
6. Read the result aloud mentally. If it sounds like a press release, rewrite again.

## Russian Defaults

- Prefer normal spoken-written Russian over translated English syntax.
- Use "я" when describing agent work if it is simpler than passive constructions.
- Avoid bureaucratic fillers: "данный", "осуществлять", "является", "следует отметить", "в рамках".
- Avoid forced internet slang unless the user uses it first.
- Short punchy sentences are fine; do not make every sentence short.

## Before Delivering

- Would a reader know what changed, what to do, or what to think?
- Does any line sound like filler from a generic AI answer?
- Are claims grounded in provided facts or clearly marked as inference?
- Is the final paragraph doing work, or merely bowing?
